I earned my Master’s of Science from New Mexico State University in 2024. I'm passionate about computer science, education, and learning. My dream is to attain a professorship after completing my Ph.D. My research interests are in shared play experiences. Currently, I am investigating how computer games lead players to share cooperative and collaborative experiences, and how that informs meaningful social and design outcomes. Throughout my undergraduate and graduate studies, I've also been passionate about low level computing. Some of my favorite courses were Compilers, Operating Systems I & II, Parallel Programming, Machine Programming, and Automata.
